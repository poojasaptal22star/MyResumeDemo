Welcome to Laundry Mart / Laundry Wallah

Laundry is the washing of clothing and other textiles, and, more broadly, their drying and ironing as well. Laundry has been part of history since humans began to wear clothes, so the methods by which different cultures have dealt with this universal human need are of interest to several branches of scholarship. The word "laundry" is derived from the Latin word "lavare", meaning "to wash". The word "laundry" is also used to refer to a place where clothes are washed, such as a laundromat or a laundry room.
